# Cirkl Brand Guidelines

## Brand Promise
"Connecting Authentically, Growing Intelligently"

## Voice & Tone
- **Authentic**: Direct, honest, no corporate speak
- **Empathetic**: Understanding, supportive, human
- **Intelligent**: Insightful without being condescending
- **Optimistic**: Positive about real connections
- **Respectful**: Privacy-conscious, consent-driven

## Visual Identity

### Logo Treatment
- Orbital rings representing connections
- Glassmorphic effects on overlays
- Animated on interaction (subtle pulse)
- Adaptive to light/dark modes

### Iconography
- SF Symbols as base
- Custom icons for:
  - Physical verification (QR/NFC)
  - Orbital connections
  - AI assistant
  - Privacy controls
  - Trust indicators
  - Connection strength

### Motion Design
- Duration: 200-400ms standard
- Easing: Spring physics preferred
- Micro-interactions on all touchpoints
- Loading states with orbital spinner
- Parallax depth for cards
- Gesture-driven navigation

## Key Differentiators
1. **Zero Fake Profiles**: Visual trust indicators
2. **Physical First**: QR/NFC prominently featured
3. **AI Empathy**: Conversational, not transactional
4. **Privacy Power**: User controls front and center
5. **Quality Metrics**: Depth over breadth visualization

## UI Copy Guidelines
- Action buttons: Verb + Object ("Share Moment")
- Confirmations: Explain impact ("This will...")
- Errors: Solution-focused ("Try..." not "Can't...")
- Empty states: Encouraging actions
- Onboarding: Progressive disclosure
- Success messages: Celebrate authentically

## Color Psychology
- **Blue (#007AFF)**: Trust, stability, professionalism
- **Glass White**: Purity, transparency, openness
- **Authentic Red (#FF6B6B)**: Energy, passion, real connections
- **Success Teal (#4ECDC4)**: Growth, balance, wellbeing
- **Warning Yellow (#FFE66D)**: Attention, caution, awareness

## Accessibility Commitments
- WCAG AAA compliance target
- VoiceOver optimization
- Haptic feedback for key actions
- High contrast mode support
- Reduced motion alternatives
- Dyslexia-friendly fonts option
- Screen reader announcements
- Keyboard navigation support

## Marketing Angles
- "Your real network, not your feed"
- "Meet in person, connect forever"
- "Quality connections, not follower counts"
- "Privacy by design, trust by default"
- "AI that understands, not manipulates"

## Partnership Opportunities
- Coffee shops (meeting points)
- Co-working spaces (professional networking)
- Universities (student connections)
- Conferences (event networking)
- Local businesses (community building)
